Temperature Sensor

Measures Ambient Temperature in the range of -30°C to +80° C with a typical error of ±0.75°C in the 0°C to 80°C range.

Light Sensor

The 1142 Light sensor can measure ambient light up to 1000 lux. Roughly equivalent to the sky on an overcast day

Humidity Sensor

Measures Relative Humidity from 10% to 95% with a typical error of ±2%RH at 55% RH and fast response

Power Switch
